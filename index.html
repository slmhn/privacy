Overview

英语思维模型 ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. The App:

  • Does not collect personal information
  • Does not track your usage or behavior
  • Does not use analytics or advertising SDKs
  • Does not transmit any data to external servers
  • Runs fully offline — no internet connection required

Data Storage

All data generated by the App is stored locally on your device only and is never shared with us or any third party.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party services, advertising networks, or analytics platforms.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Since we collect no data at all, the App is safe for users of all ages.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
